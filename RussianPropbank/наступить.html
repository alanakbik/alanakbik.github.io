Predicate: наступить

Roleset id: наступить.01, come, motion Source: [come.01]

Roles:

        A1: entity in motion / 'comer'
        A2: extent -- rare)
        A3: start point -- also rare)
        A4: end point
        AM: direction (usually 'up' or 'down')
