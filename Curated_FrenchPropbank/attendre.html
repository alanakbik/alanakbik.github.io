attendre.01: attendre qn/qch

Roles:

        A1: wait-er (not waiter, that's the other sense)
        A2: thing waited for
        Am: period of time waited through

Example:

        A1: Naimi
        AM-NEG: renonce
        Rel: attendre
        A2: Matti
        AM-TMP: davantage

Example:

        La mort vous attend ici . "

        Death waits for you here . "

        A1: La mort
        A2: vous

        AM-LOC: ici
