Roleset id: provoquer.01, provoke, cause (us. emotional reaction) Source: [provoke.01]

provoquer.01: provoquer qn/qch

Roles:

        A0: causer
        A1: thing provoked, or reaction when separate secondary impelled agent is present
        A2: secondary impelled agent, when separate from reaction

Example:

        La victoire provoquera l' injustice ,

        Victory will provoke injustice .

        A0: La victoire
        Rel: provoquera
        A1: l' injustice

Example:

        Vous essayez de me provoquer .

        You are deliberately trying to provoke me .

        A0: Vous
        A1: me
        Rel: provoquer

Example:

        Tu vas les provoquer .

        - You 'll provoke them . You 'll tell the newspapers , that you had seen them ..

        A0: Tu
        A1: les
        Rel: provoquer

Example:

        Si l' un d' entre vous provoque un incident qui met en danger notre mission , je ferai regretter à cet homme d' avoir vu le jour .

        Let any one of you provoke an incident which endangers it , and I shall cause that man to curse his mother for giving him birth .

        A0: l' un d' entre vous
        Rel: provoque
        A1: un incident qui met en danger notre mission

Example:

        Il m' a provoqué .

        - He provoked me .

        A0: Il
        A1: m'
        Rel: provoqué
