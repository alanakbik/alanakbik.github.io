citer.01: citer qn/qch

Roles:

        A0: quoter
        A1: quoted: speaker
        A2: quote: utterance or attribute of arg1
        A3: what the quote is about

Example:

        Il vous citait souvent .

        - He used to quote you a lot .

        A0: Il
        A2: vous
        Rel: citait
        A1: souvent

        AM-ADV: viens
        Rel: citer
